About our Hotel

Hotel Transylvania was built in 1989. This hotel has served the people for more than 3 decades and is one of the most luxurious hotels in town.

The hotel's infrastructure is inspired from Byzantine Architecture. Many people including celebrities have stayed at our hotel and given it a 5-star rating. 49 spacious and smartly furnished rooms and suites with a magnificent view that let the outdoors in. All rooms offer: high-speed WiFi net access • complimentary daily newspaper and mineral water • tea/coffee maker • telephones with global direct dialing and voice mail • LCD TV • DVD (on request) • satellite television • mini bar • comfortable mattress • well appointed bathroom.
